ROBBERY / THEFT CASES

Three new cases have been reported in the last 24 hours in the country.

The whole report of the case including the statements from the police officer incharge of the respective cases has been given below.

Jewellery worth Rs 80 lakhs was seized from a Police constable in Kolkata last night. There was a raid at Mr. Chakhraborathy's house where the jewellery was found. A nearby jewelleryshop had a break in just few days before and when inquired with the jewellery shop owner, it was fond out that the jewellery were from his shop. The jewellery shop was broken open using a gas cutter and the gas cutter was found in Mr. Chakhraborathy's house.

A gang that stole pickup vans from parking lots, and sold them after altering them was nabbed by Boisar unit of the local crime branch. Seven men were arrested. The accused confessed for 64 cases of vehicle theft from Palghar, Thane, Gujarat, Dadra and Nahar Haveli. The gang would keep a watch for vans along roads and those coming into parking lots. With duplicate keys, they would steal the vehicle at night and take them to a garage in Bhiwandi. The accussed were booked for theft and forgery among the other sections of the IPC. Police seized 27 vehicles from them.

City Police on Wednesday arrested a 23-year old man, who allegedly burgled three schools and a church in Paneer near Mangaluru. Another accused is absconding, said police. The arrested has been identified as Rohan, a resident of KC Nagar. He was produces before a court and remanded in judicial custody.
